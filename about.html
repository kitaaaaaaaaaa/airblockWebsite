This page will give a comprehensive dive into the mechanics and wonders of how it works. It will looks at the various forms that Airblock can be transformed into along with each forms actually works by looking at how the company has designed it to allow it to move, fly and drive.
Drone

Airblock has 3 main forms that it can turn into, the main form is the airblock drone that consists of a body attached with 6 propellers and when activated it moves by using all propellers to get lift and varying amounts of force to allow it to tilt and fly in a certain direction.

Hovercraft

The second main form is the hovercraft that also uses all 6 propellers, however 2 are facing up and the 2 on each side are facing away from each other, this being done on both sides. This design is then placed in a styrofoam body and moves by using the 2 facing down to take off and generate lift. Then the propellers will be used to generate thrust to move left and right.

Spinner

The last main form is the spin top. This is a fun and comedic like form and just used to spin in circles. This design is composed by using 3 propellers facing the same direction with a gap in between each propeller. When activated, lights attached on the propeller form a circle of colour due to the incredible speed.
