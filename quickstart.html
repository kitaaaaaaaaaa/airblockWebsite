How to use the Airblock

1

Install the mobile app

2

Plug in the battery with the included Micro USB cable.

3

Port numbers

How to Setup the Drone

1

Connect the fan with the red foot pad to port 1.

2

Connect the other fan modules to the Master Controller, alternating the fan colours.

3

Install the charged battery into the Master Controller, ensuring the contact pins on the battery match with the pins on the port. The lights on top should turn white, indicating the device is in standby mode.

4

Follow guide instructions in the app to connect to the Drone.

How to Setup the Hovercraft

1

Install the battery in the Master Controller, ensuring the contact pins on the battery match with the pins on the port.

2

Install the guards on the fan modules, only on top for ports 1 and 4 and on both top and bottom for ports 2, 3, 5, and 6.

3

Connect the fan module with the red foot to port 1 and a black fan to port 4.

4

Fit Master Controller module with fans connected into the Hovercraft hull.

5

Connect the other fan modules to the master controller, alternating the fan colours, positioning the feet facing inwards.

6

Follow guide instructions in the app to connect to the Hovercraft.
